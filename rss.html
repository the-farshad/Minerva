Completed-this-week tasks, generated locally from your Sheet.

Minerva can publish your completed-this-week tasks as a public RSS feed in your own Drive — same architecture as the iCal feed: client-side generation, public Drive file, anyone can subscribe.

Click Generate / refresh feed to (re-)publish. After publishing, paste the URL into your RSS reader.
